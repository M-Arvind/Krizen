3D PRINTING
Register

ABOUT

The main objective of this session is to give the participants an idea about 3d printing technology, its history, and various methods of making a 3d printer. After this session, the participants will be able to convert a CAD file into a physical part using a 3D printer

DETAILS

This event requires the Participants to attend a 2hr Workshop regarding the basics and the operations of a 3D printer and the products which could be printed using it. This session will be productive for the attendees and will enable them with the knowledge regarding the basics of 3D printing as mentioned above. Metha T managing partner @technojinn solutions will be providing us with the required necessities for the workshop.
