3D PRINTING
Register

ABOUT

The main objective of this session is to give the participants an idea about 3d printing technology, its history, and various methods of making a 3d printer. After this session, the participants will be able to convert a CAD file into a physical part using a 3D printer

DETAILS

This event requires the Participants to attend a 2hr Workshop regarding the basics and the operations of a 3D printer and the products which could be printed using it. This session will be productive for the attendees and will enable them with the knowledge regarding the basics of 3D printing as mentioned above. Metha T managing partner @technojinn solutions will be providing us with the required necessities for the workshop.

INTERNET OF THINGS
Register

ABOUT

The main objective of this event is to give the participants an introduction to IoT and various types and methods in creating an IoT product. The Internet of things describes the network of physical objects that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies to connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the Internet

DETAILS

This event will be of a duration of 2 hours in which the participants will equip themselves with a brief introduction to the technology IoT and various applications of it that we use in our daily life. This will be a fulfilled session for the attendees who want to learn this tech since they will be able to understand every aspect, terminology, and concept of IoT at the end.
