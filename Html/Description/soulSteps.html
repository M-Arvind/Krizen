SOUL STEPS

Brochure

ABOUT

An extravaganza that sets one's sight on the aspects of dancing. The hands you spin, legs you sway, neck you whirl conveys your emotion. Move and step with your soul for conveying your emotion through all means. A fun war which shall determine the best of all, on the event days. This ecstasy makes you entertained, feel, and occupy the best movements of you. Engage and enjoy yourself through this episode by exploring your skill

DETAILS

Round 1

This is a solo event, where the first round is a screening round. The shining participants from the screening round will be qualified for the final round. In the screening round the participants can submit their dance video of time range between 57-59 seconds of any songs, through the google forms that are shared to them via WhatsApp. In case of crossing the time limit, the participantâ€™s response will not be considered, the participants who are getting selected for the final round will get their participation certificate. The results of the screening round will be published on the next day of submission.

Round 2

The participants qualified for the final round will be given a property on the day of announcing the result for the screening round. The participants should dance with the mentioned property with the same time limit of screening round and send the video through google forms shared to them. The best use of property, the toughness, and innovation of moves through the property will be recognized. The deadline for submitting the videos will be after 2 days from the screening round. The best of 2 from the participants will be recognised.

Date & Timings

Round 1 : Submission within 08:00 PM on 11.09.2021

Round 2 : Submission within 11:59 PM on 14.09.2021

Last date for registration is 10.09.2021 within 6:00 PM
