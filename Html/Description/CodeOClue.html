ABOUT

A contest that brings out your coding skills and your speed. Use your Coding skills, Time management to find the right question and code your way to the top. This is not just about coding, this is about how you can quickly identify the clues by paying attention to small details and make the right decisions before it’s too late. Remember, the clues will be hidden in plain sight. This is a test of your INSTINCTS.

DETAILS

  • The participants will have 5 gateways and each gateway consists of 4 questions. There will be a total of 20 coding questions and 5 clues to find the famous movie or famous personality or world-famous event.
  • All 5 clues will be hidden in the 20 questions and clues might be a year or a word from the sentence or a name related to the final answer. Only one question in a gateway has the clue and on solving that question only one test case will be “VISIBLE” and that test case will give them the clue. The participants are allowed to move to the next gateway only if they have the clue from the particular gateway.
  • The participants will be required to submit the clue in a google form which contains the link to the next Gateway. Once they have all the five clues, a Submission form will be given to them to submit all the 5 clues and the Hidden answer in 120 mins.
  • The first participant to find the hidden answer is the winner. If there are more than one, the fastest wins. There will be only one round and one winner and one runnerup. This contest will be conducted on Hacker-earth platforms. The questions are not limited to any coding language. Participants just need to have basic knowledge of coding.
