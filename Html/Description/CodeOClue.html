ABOUT

A contest that brings out your coding skills, spontaneous thinking, and to test your general knowledge. The real challenge in CodeoClue is not coding, it is time management and thinking out of the box. This is not just about coding, this is about how you can quickly identify the clues by paying attention to small details and make the right decisions before itâ€™s too late. This is a test of your INSTINCTS.

DETAILS

  • The participants will have 5 gateways and each gateway consists of 4 questions. There will be a total of 20 coding questions and 5 clues to find the famous movie or famous personality or world-famous event.
  • All 5 clues will be hidden in the 20 questions and clues might be a year or a word from the sentence or a name related to the final answer. Only one question in a gateway has the clue and on solving the question a pop-up will give them the clue.
  • The first participant to find the Hidden name or the sentence is the winner. If there are more than one, the fastest wins. There will be only one round and one winner and one runner-up.
  • The participants will be allowed to move to the next gateway only if they have the clue from the particular gateway. This contest will be conducted on online coding platforms. The questions are not limited to any coding language. Participants just need to have basic knowledge of coding.
