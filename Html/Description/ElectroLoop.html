ABOUT

A contest to test the knowledge of the participants in the field of electricals and electronics. This contest also tests their ability to swiftly design a circuit in the most efficient way possible, with limited resources.

DETAILS

Round 1

The first round is an elimination round, the participants qualifying in this round will qualify to the next round (final round). In this round, the participants will be provided with images of a number of electrical and electronic components. They should identify the uses and features of the given components. This round will be conducted via google forms or online proctoring portal, which will have a time limit of 30 mins. In case the time limit is exceeded, the answers will be auto submitted. The participants who qualify for the second round will be given a participation certificate.

Round 2

In the second round (final round), the previously qualified participants will be asked to design a circuit for performing the given tasks. The participants should design the circuit through the platform of their desire, but the submissions should be in the format of .PNG or .JPEG . The submission process will be carried out through google forms (link will be provided along with the questions). The participants should submit their circuit designs within 2 hours. The most efficient and fastest submissions will be considered for evaluation. The top 2 scorers among the participants will be awarded.
