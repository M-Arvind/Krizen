MECHANIQUIZ

ABOUT

A contest to bring out the esteemed mind of the future engineers in the field of Mechanical engineering through series of questions with the sole purpose of enhancing their knowledge towards technical aspects especially with machines.

DETAILS

  • We compiled a series of objective questions, based on the GATE syllabus, with the sole purpose of bringing out the knowledge of contestants in the mechanical field.
  • The first round consists of 50 Questions. It will be a screening round. Questions in this round will be from machine aptitude, thermodynamics and manufacturing engineering. The time limit for this event will be 60 minutes. The top 30 participants from the first round will be screened and will move to the second round.
  • The second round contains 30 questions. From those 20 participants, the top two performers will be recognized and awarded.
  • During round one the questions will be shuffled in a random manner whereas during round two each questions will be given 120 seconds; once time runs out it automatically moves to the next question after then he/she will not be able to go back to the previous question
