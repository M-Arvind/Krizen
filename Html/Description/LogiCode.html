ABOUT

This event is to test your programming knowledge and your problem-solving skills. The event topics will be based on arrays, strings, pattern printing, pointers, matrices, and data structure. This event will raise your competitive spirit and will be helpful in testing the space complexity and time complexity of your code. Challenge your efficiency and confidence in coding knowledge.

DETAILS

Round 1

This event will be conducted through online coding platform. This event consists of two rounds. Each round contains five problems. Languages supported for this event are C, C++, JAVA, Python. In the first round, the problems will be based on arrays and strings. The duration of the first round will be 60 minutes, after the time limit exceeds the answers will be auto-submitted for both the first and second round. Whoever completes all the problems in the first round will be qualified for the second round. Participants who qualify for the second round will be given a participation certificate.

Round 2

In the second round, the problems will be based on Data structures and arrays. The duration of this round will be 120 minutes. The participant who scores a highest mark in the second round will be the winner. If two or more participants score the same mark the winner will be chosen by the time and space constraints of the solution. The top 2 participants will be awarded.

Date & Timings

Round 1 : Commences on 14.09.2021 @ 11:00 AM

Round 2 : Commences on 15.09.2021 @ 10:00 AM
