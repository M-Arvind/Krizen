ABOUT

Creativity is the word of action and the output can be a beautiful vision.VideoVisions brings out that hovering creativity in you through videoediting skills. The radical message that you tend to convey through your video can be attained by a incredible way of storytelling . Music gives a intense feel to your video to be wholesome. The trending Transition editorials usually attract this generation, as they create a exquisite touch to your video. Montage of various videos combined together with a good screenplay, music and transition, VideoVision let you encompass all these elements to explore your video editing skills and to develope them.

DETAILS

Round 1

The first round is the acquaintance round. The participants are asked to send a video file that lasts for a minute (or) within, in accordance with the topic given. The best of the videos from this round will be qualified for the second round.

Round 2

The participants qualified for the second round “Montage in time” are asked to create a montage video with various transitions required for the given topic. The music used for the videos (for both first and second rounds) can be either a song or bgm. EFX audio can also be included. The best of the videos with on-point transition, bgm, and screenplay in accordance with the given topic will be recognized. The participants are asked to send their videos through google forms shared with them via WhatsApp. The video must contain a watermark of the participant’s name and access number given to them. The deadline for submitting the video will be on the next day of the first round. The participants submitting their response before the deadline will be given a participation certificate and the best two will be recognised.
