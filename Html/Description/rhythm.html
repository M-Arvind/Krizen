ABOUT

Music find their way onto secret places of the soul.Rhythm allows the participants to uncover the talent in music. Singing makes everything more fun and lightens the heart, it causes the mind to focus and body to relax. Life seems to go on without effort when we are filled with music. This is not only meant for professionals but also to bring out the confidence in aspiring singers.

DETAILS

This event consists of a single round. The participants are asked to record a video of them singing without using auto tune or karoke and the video should not exceed one minute. Google form link will be provided to all the participants through whatsApp and submission date will be announced very soon after the registration. Submission after the due time will not be entertained. All the participants will be given a participation certificate. The participants with booming voice, good control on high notes and great overall performance will be awarded with the winning certificate (best of two participants).
