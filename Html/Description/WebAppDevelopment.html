WEB/APP DEVELOPMENT
Register

ABOUT

Experience a fresh and modern Web/App Development event where attendees can show-off their coding and design skills. This event is organized for developers by developers, and strives to foster the ideal social and learning experience. Featured topics include languages and clean code, database, team performance, devops and much more. Expand your network, connect and collaborate with peers from around the world, and discover new ways to take your code and application architecture to the next level.

DETAILS

A specific topic will be provided to the participants a week before the event. Participants will have to build a Website or an application based on the theme provided. Participants cannot build both. Two developers who prevail will be awarded accordingly.


  • All design elements, code, etc. for your project must be created during the event.
  • Third party tools and frameworks are allowed as well as your normal tooling.
  • There are no restrictions as of the coding lanugage you are using.
  • Participants can submit only one entry.
  • Participants can form a team containing maximum of FOUR members.
  • If you are participating as a team, only the team leader has to register.
  • Participants should also give a brief discription about their website or application.
  • Submission time will be announced when the theme is released.

Judging Criteria

Participants will be judged based on the following criteria.

  • Realistic Capability
  • Level of Innovation
  • Design
  • Bugginess
