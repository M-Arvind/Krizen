ABOUT

In this competition, competitors will be tested on various competencies that are essential in design aspects for engineers. These skills include drafting 2D technical drawings, 3D models in software like CAD, CATIA, CREO. This plateform will help you enhance your technical skill and boost your confidence.

DETAILS

  • Cad fest event will have two rounds. In the first round, the participants will be given a 2D diagram and the best 15 submissions will be qualified for the final round.

  • In the second round, the participants will be given two views of any diagram and the participant will have to draft the 3D sketch of the particular diagram the top two submissions of the final round will be awarded.
  • If the number of participants is large a screening round before the first round will be held. The screening round is a quiz competition that is based on the basics of engineering drafting.
  • Link for the competition will be sent 15 minutes prior to the competition in the WhatsApp group. After two hours the submission only from the registered email address will be accepted. The submissions must be in a part file format only.
